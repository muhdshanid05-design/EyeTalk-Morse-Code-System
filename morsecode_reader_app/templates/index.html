ABOUT EYE TALK

Empowering Communication Through Innovation

At EYE TALK, we’re not just developing technology — we’re redefining the way people communicate. Our mission is to give a voice to individuals with severe motor disabilities through AI-powered eye-tracking and Morse code translation, enabling them to express thoughts, emotions, and needs effortlessly.

Built with compassion and advanced technology, EYE TALK bridges the gap between human intention and digital communication. By converting simple eye blinks or gaze movements into Morse code, we transform them into meaningful words and sentences — bringing independence, dignity, and connection to those who need it most.

Founded by innovators passionate about accessibility and human potential, EYE TALK represents a leap forward in assistive communication technology. We combine cutting-edge AI, machine learning, and real-time eye tracking to deliver a solution that is both accurate, reliable, and life-changing.

Together, we’re building a future where every eye movement can speak volumes.

Innovation at Every Blink

At EYE TALK, our commitment to excellence means rethinking what assistive technology can do. We combine human-centered design with advanced research so that small, natural eye movements become robust, reliable communication channels. The result is a system that’s precise enough for everyday use and resilient enough for real-life situations.

From adaptive machine learning models that improve with use to low-latency eye-tracking that works in varied lighting and seating conditions, our platform brings together practical engineering and cutting-edge AI. The modular design lets caregivers and clinicians tailor sensitivity, timing, and output formats to each user’s needs — so the technology adapts to the person, not the other way around.

Experience assistive communication that’s not just functional, but empowering. With EYE TALK, users gain faster message composition, hands-free control of connected devices, and seamless integration with everyday communication tools — helping restore independence, dignity, and connection.
